## 2. Morality System: Humanism vs. Transhumanism
The core conflict of the Sun Eater setting is the definition of "Humanity."

### üèõÔ∏è Humanism (Positive Axis)
- **Philosophy**: Preservation of the natural human form. Rejection of high-AI and invasive cybernetics.
- **Support**: Backed by the Chantry.
- **Bonuses**: High Charisma, Defense, and "Faith" based abilities.
- **Weapons**: Elegant ballistics, energy shields, and matter-swords.

### üß¨ Transhumanist (Negative Axis)
- **Philosophy**: Evolution through technology. Cybernetics, genetic splicing, and neural uploads.
- **Support**: Underground research cells and radical factions.
- **Bonuses**: Increased Intelligence, Dexterity, and Speed. Access to "The Quiet" (reality-warping).
- **Weapons**: Neural-link weaponry, nanite swarms, and bio-warfare.

---

## 3. Power Scaling: The Four Tiers
Progression follows a logarithmic curve from Level 1 to 50.

1.  **Initiate (Lvl 1-10)**: Basic ballistics and physical training.
2.  **Knight (Lvl 11-25)**: Solar-matter weapons and minor genetic enhancements.
3.  **Champion (Lvl 26-40)**: Advanced neural links and command over small squads.
4.  **Palatine (Lvl 41-50)**: Masters of the Quiet. Capable of bending gravity and time.

---

## 4. Combat Physics
- **Resolution**: d20 + Modifiers vs. Armor Class (AC).
- **Initiative**: Determined by `d20 + Dexterity + Alertness`.
- **Resources**: Stamina (Physical) and Neural Strain (Mental).
